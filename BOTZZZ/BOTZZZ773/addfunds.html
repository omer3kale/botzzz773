Frequently Asked Questions

What is Payeer?

Payeer is a secure and popular e-wallet system that allows you to make instant online payments. It supports multiple currencies and offers low transaction fees.

How long does it take to add funds?

Funds are typically added to your account instantly after successful payment. In rare cases, it may take up to 5 minutes for the balance to reflect.

What is the minimum deposit amount?

The minimum deposit amount is $5.00. You can add any amount above this minimum using preset amounts or entering a custom value.

Are there any fees?

A small processing fee of 2.5% is applied to all transactions to cover payment gateway costs. The total amount including fees is shown before payment.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! All payments are processed through Payeer's secure gateway with industry-standard encryption. We never store your payment credentials.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the instant nature of our services, added funds are non-refundable. Please ensure you're adding the correct amount before proceeding.
